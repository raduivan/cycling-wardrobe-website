Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information we collect

1.1 Wardrobe data

Stored locally or synced when you sign in: item names, type, season tags, and condition flags (windproof, waterproof, thermal, etc.). No photos or media are uploaded.

1.2 Planner inputs & route data

When you use Weather Assist or the Manual Planner, we process: location input (city or coordinates), latitude/longitude from GPX files or route start points, ride inputs (temperature, wind, rain, humidity, duration, intensity, speed), GPX-derived stats (distance, elevation, estimated time), weather results and recommendations.

If you sign in, we may store recent ride sessions (weather_sessions), uploaded routes and GPX metadata (routes), and generated kit recommendations (recommendations) so you can reuse routes/devices and maintain continuity across sessions.

1.3 Account data (optional)

If you create an account, we store your email address to sync wardrobe items, preferences, routes, and ride sessions across devices.

1.4 Diagnostics & telemetry

To improve stability and understand feature use, we collect planner interaction events (planner_events) and error logs (app_logs). These may include timestamps, lat/lon used in a request, route point counts, and request context. They may be linked to your account when you are signed in. We do not collect advertising IDs, third-party analytics SDKs, or behavioral profiles.

2. How we use the data

  • Generate kit recommendations based on your wardrobe and ride inputs.
  • Compute weather forecasts using your chosen location or GPX route.
  • Sync wardrobe, preferences, routes, and session history when signed in.
  • Improve reliability via planner events and error logs.
  • Respond to support requests you send.

We do not show ads, sell data, or share information for marketing.

3. Where your data goes (processing)

3.1 Supabase (EU region)

Used for account data, synced wardrobe items, routes, GPX metadata, weather sessions, and telemetry. All communication uses HTTPS; data is encrypted at rest.

3.2 Weather API provider

To fetch forecasts, we send latitude/longitude and ride parameters to our weather provider (e.g., Open-Meteo) solely to return weather data.

3.3 Email support

If you contact support, your email may include GPX files, screenshots, or technical logs you choose to provide. We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

4. Storage & retention

4.1 On your device

Wardrobe items, preferences, and planner inputs are stored locally. You can delete them via Settings → Maintenance.

4.2 In the cloud (if signed in)

Synced wardrobe items, routes, sessions, and recommendations remain until you delete them in-app or contact support. Telemetry/logs are retained for operations and periodically purged.

5. Your choices & rights

  • Use the app without an account to keep data on your device.
  • Clear local or cloud data via Settings → Maintenance or by emailing support.
  • Request access, correction, deletion, or export of cloud data.
  • GDPR/EEA/UK: you may access, correct, delete, restrict processing, and port your data; we respond within 30 days.
  • Withdraw consent by signing out or uninstalling the app.

6. Children

Cycling Wardrobe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used the app, contact us and we will remove the data.

7. Contact

Email support@cyclingwardrobe.com with privacy questions or data requests. If reporting an issue, you may include screenshots or GPX files to help us reproduce it.
