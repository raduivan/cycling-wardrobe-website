Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

What we collect

We only collect the information you enter inside the Cycling Wardrobe app:

  • Wardrobe items, seasons, and notes you type.
  • Rider preferences such as typical speed and rain tolerance.
  • GPX metadata (route distance, duration) when you import a file.

How we use it

Data is used solely to produce weather-aware kit recommendations and to sync your wardrobe between devices if you choose to sign in.

On-device vs. cloud

All data stays on-device by default. If you create an account, we store encrypted copies on Supabase so your wardrobe can sync across devices.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers. Analytics are limited to anonymous aggregates for crash diagnostics.

Retention & deletion

You can export or delete all data via Settings → Maintenance inside the app. Deleting removes both local storage and the Supabase copy.

Contact

Email support@cyclingwardrobe.app with privacy questions or deletion requests.
