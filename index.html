Why it matters

Ever started a ride and thought: “Am I overdressed? Will I freeze on the descent?” Cycling Wardrobe gives you a confident kit plan before you clip in — so you ride more comfortably in any weather.

Key benefits

  • Ride ready, whatever the weather. Your kit adapts to cold mornings, windy ridges, and surprise showers.
  • Based on your actual gear. Recommendations use the items from your personal wardrobe.
  • Built around your route. Import a GPX so the weather and conditions reflect where you actually ride.
  • Fast and simple. No overthinking — get a clear clothing suggestion in just a few taps.

What you can do today

Weather Assist

Import your GPX route or pick a city and get a kit recommendation that accounts for temperature, wind, rain and time of day.

Manual planner

Prefer manual control? Enter ride conditions like temperature, wind, rain, effort, and duration, and see what to wear for any scenario.

Wardrobe manager

Add your jerseys, jackets, tights and accessories. Tag them by season and capabilities so recommendations match your real gear.

Cloud sync

Sign in if you want to sync wardrobe and preferences across devices. Stay fully offline if you prefer — the app still works.

Data & privacy

Your data stays yours. Wardrobe items and preferences stay on your device unless you choose to sign in.

If you create an account, we only sync what’s needed to keep your wardrobe and settings available across devices. We don’t run ads, we don’t sell your data, and we don’t share your information with third parties.
