Why it matters

Ever started a ride and thought: “Am I overdressed? Will I freeze on the descent?” Cycling Wardrobe gives you a confident kit plan before you clip in — so you ride more comfortably in any weather.

What you can do today

Weather Assist

Import a GPX or pick a city and get a tailored kit recommendation that accounts for temperature, wind, and rain.

Manual planner

Fine-tune every detail—temperature, effort, duration—to experiment with different ride scenarios.

Wardrobe manager

Track your kit with seasons, notes, and range. Export / import JSON so testing builds never lose progress.

Cloud sync

Sign in (optional) to keep wardrobe and preferences backed up and synced across devices through our secure cloud infrastructure.

How we handle your data

  • Wardrobe items, preferences, and saved plans stay on-device unless you sign in.
  • When signed in, data syncs to our managed cloud backend using encrypted connections.
  • We do not run ads or share your data with third parties.
  • You can export or delete everything at any time in Settings › Maintenance.
