Support

Last updated: January 2025

How to reach us

Email support@cyclingwardrobe.com. We aim to reply within 2 business days.

What to include

  • A short description of the issue and the screen you were on.
  • Your TestFlight build version (e.g., 0.2.0) and platform (iOS/Android).
  • Screenshots or a GPX file if the issue involves routes or weather sampling.
  • Approximate time/timezone the issue occurred (helps find logs).

Common requests we can handle

  • Account or sync issues (wardrobe, routes, or history not updating).
  • Weather/route sampling problems (GPX import, location lookups, forecast errors).
  • Data requests: export, correction, or deletion of cloud-stored data.
  • Bug reports and feature feedback from TestFlight builds.
